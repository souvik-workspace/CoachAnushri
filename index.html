What is Glow & Grow concept?

We discover & learn few aspects of our personal growth & development while having open discussions with our friends & colleagues.

One such discussion helped me from a candid interaction with my friend Indu Punj.

The “Glow and Grow” technique is a powerful method for self-reflection and personal growth.

🎯It encourages individuals to reflect on their strengths (glows) and areas for improvement (grows)in a constructive and growth-oriented manner.

Reflect on what you do well. This could be your skills, achievements, or positive interactions.

Celebrate Successes Everytime : Acknowledge and celebrate your accomplishments, no matter how small. This boosts confidence and motivation.

Positive Feedback : Seek and embrace positive feedback from others. It helps reinforce your strengths and areas where you shine.

What is the common fundamental concept in psychology, philosophy, and spirituality?

It is this statement - "Our perception shapes our reality"

Indeed a profound one🎖️

It suggests that our individual experiences, beliefs, and attitudes influence how we interpret and interact with the world.

✴️This concept is supported by various theories and principles:

🎖️The Power of Perspective - Our unique viewpoint and experiences shape our understanding.

🎖️Confirmation Bias - We tend to seek information that confirms our existing beliefs.

🎖️The Law of Attraction - Our thoughts and emotions attract similar energies.

🎖️Neuro-plasticity - Our brain reorganize based on our experiences and perceptions.
